Program

The workshop lasts a full day and is organised as follows.

The morning will start with the submitted position papers. Each presenter will have 5 to 10 minutes for presenting their poster and 1 to 3 minutes for questions. Following the poster presentations, participants will draw care networks, inspired by their fieldwork or their own experience. Discussions will follow these activities to learn from the commonalities and differences between care networks.

During the afternoon, we will draw on the World Café method to stimulate discussions. We will select the most common themes raised during previous discussions and assign theme to a discussion table. We will invite participants to discuss these themes in small groups, and after some minutes, rotate to another table. Following this exercise, we will map ideas, issues, challenges, and future directions.

Participants are encouraged to join a group dinner to continue discussions and to build relationships with people that share their interests around care networks.
